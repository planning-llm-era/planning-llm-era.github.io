# Resources 

Below we provide a non-exhaustive list of resources. 

### Tools 

- LLM-driven Planning Model library kit [L2P](https://github.com/AI-Planning/l2p)
- MACQ Visualizer: A Holistic View of Model Acquisition Techniques, [MACQ](MACQ.planning.domains)
- A gym env to interact with any PDDL domain, [PDDLGym](https://github.com/tomsilver/pddlgym)

### Benchmarks 

- Datasets for core reasoining abilities of LMs, ACPBench and ACPBench-Hard. ([website](https://ibm.github.io/ACPBench/), [hugging-face](https://huggingface.co/datasets/ibm/ACPBench))
- PlanBench
- TRAC
- AutoPlanBench
- NaturalPlan
- TravelPlanner


### Previous Tutorials

- Tutorial on **Action Model Acquisition**, [ICAPS 2023](https://icaps23.icaps-conference.org/program/tutorials/model/), Christian Muise and Tathagata Chakraborti
- Tutorial on **Planning in Natural Language with Foundational Models (PLAN-FM)**, [AAAI 2025](https://plan-fm.github.io/2025/), Harsha Kokel, Shirin Sohrabi, Soham Dan, and Manling Li


### Community Resources

- A wiki for planning and PDDL, [planning.wiki](https://planning.wiki)
- The Planning community [slack](https://planning.wiki/slack)
- Collection of tools for working with planning domains, [Planning.Domains](http://planning.domains)
- Community space for AI Planning repositories on [github](https://github.com/ai-planning)
